Our client, Monika, is the daily manages of the cafe and has been working at the cafe for a year. The cafe has been open for 20 years, before that is was used as a hotel. The house is from around 1860. Monika will send us more background info on the cafe on mail later for the website.

Purpose and Goals

The goal of the website is to give the cafe a more profeccional look, and to reach out to more visitors. They would like to reach out to international customers, mostly from Spain and Germany. But also students who are new in town. They also wanted to get a good overview over event that are happening in the future. Both big events like exhibitions and smaller thing like when they are open on sundays. Displaying contact info and menu was also important for them.

Audience

She mostly has older customers, but also students and some internationals. She would like the website to be accessable and readable for all, so would be ideal to have the possibility to switch at least parts of the site to english. The older customers will most likely not use the website, so it will mainly be for younger customers under 60 years. They will most likely use it for menu, contact info and actually finding the cafe.

Forside

The Forside is going to contain som basic information about the cafe and a picture of the place. The Forside is going to look appealing so that people would like to visit the cafe

Om oss

Om oss is going to be a page about the cafe, when you hover over the navbar on "Om oss" it's also going to showcase the "Historie" as well.

Historie

Historie is going to be a page about the history behind the house. Monika will send us more info about the history behind the house later.

Meny

Meny is going to be a page with the menu. Monika would like to switch the menu herself, so we're considering to show the menu as a pdf to make it easy for her.

Arrangement/kalender

This is going to be some sort of display of different activities and happenings at the house where the cafe is located

Utleige/selskap

Here you can book the house for events. Send a request of booking (mail e.g.)

Galleri

A gallery with photos, e.g. from events or food and so on.

Functional and Non-functional requirements

  • Functional requirements
    • Customer should be able to enter in feeback into a feedback form, and send it.
    • Link to other webpages, mainly related to events that take place at Fru Svendsen.
    • Live Google map of location, with the opportunity to zoom.
    • Instagram
    • Facebook
    • Trip-Advisor
  • Non-functional requirements
    • Navbar so you can get to every page within 2-3 clicks.
    • Jpeg instead of png (download time less then 1 sec)
    • Want the "guloker" to be presented in the website. (hex code is #CC7722)
    • Do not have a database, so it is not dependent on other services, making it faster to load.
    • Trying to make the maintenance as small as posible since they do not have a lot experience with html/css.

Specific Preferences

  • Fru Svendsen would like a minimalistic design, but also prefer their brandcolor on the webpage "guloker", which is the color on their house. (hex code is #CC7722)
  • Fru svendsn have specifically told us that they would like to be able to modify the content of the website. Monika have used html code before (20 years since), but they can also pay us later if they need to update the website.
  • They do also have some international custumers, so the menu should be in both norwegian and english. And they prefer that the international custumers can find the website, or at least the location of the cafe.
  • They do also need a upgraded logo and visual profile. They want the house to be a part of the logo since it is their distinguishing mark.

Final Location

Monika will buy a domain for fru Svendsen, and will cover the hosting fees. Monika wants to take care of the website after we deliveres it, and make changes herself. We have offered to help if needed.
